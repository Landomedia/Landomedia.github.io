FAQ

Everything you need to know before you get started

How much will I get paid per post?

For every 100 followers you have you will get paid 1 dollar, so if you have 1,000 followers, you will get paid 10 dollars per post.

How will I get contacted when you have an advertisement they would like me to post?

You will be contacted through the email address you provided when signing up.

When will I get paid for posting an advertisement?

After you have posted the advertisement for the allotted time given, email us a screenshot. Once we have received the picture, we will pay you through the method you chose when signing up.

How long will I have to post an advertisement on my account when using Kidnetwork?

Each advertisement you post will be required to stay up for two hours.

How will you choose which ads I post through Kidnetwork?

Each advertisement will be chosen based on your location and the number of followers you have.

How can I find opportunities through Kidnetwork?

Once you have signed up with Kidnetwork, we will email you with opportunities as soon as they come. Check your email frequently so you know when there is an opportunity to post an advertisement.

Can I choose what advertisement I post on my social media account?

We will send you each advertisement that you will post on your social media account. If you feel uncomfortable posting an advertisement we have given you, please let us know. You are not required to post any advertisement given to you.
